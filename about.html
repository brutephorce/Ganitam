Welcome to GANITAM

How it started?

Ganitam Institute of Maths was established by the vision and toil of Er. Piyush Patel, a Mechanical Engineering Graduate from NIT Bhopal and who have rich experience of teaching and mentoring of thousands of successful JEE Aspirants from last 8 years. Piyush Sir have experience of teaching and mentoring in prestigious institutes of India likewise Bansal Classes (Kota) & Super30 (CSRL) as senior faculty.

How it is going?

We currently stand as a forum for learning maths, with a vision to provide an ideal launch pad for serious JEE, and Board exam aspirants. Along the way to the realisation of this vision, we went beyond merely coaching for JEE and Academics. We actually started to make a difference in the way students think and approach towards Maths. As an organisation, Ganitam is not just an online platform run by an individual teacher, but a mature academic system developed and maintained by its own management and academic operations team.
